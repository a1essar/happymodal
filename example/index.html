Usage

HappyModals requires jQuery and Modernizr to run. Download latest version of jQuery and Modernizr and place them to your page.

Add HappyModals to your page.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="happymodal/happymodal.css"/>
<script src="happymodal/jquery.happymodal.min.js"></script>

Initialization happymodal plugin

  • hideCallback
